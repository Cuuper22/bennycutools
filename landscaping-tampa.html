Seasonal Maintenance Plans

Keep your landscape thriving year-round with our tailored maintenance plans, designed for the unique climate of Tampa, FL.

Tampa Seasonal Care Calendar

Summer (June - August)

  • Irrigation Optimization: Adjust for heat, water restrictions.
  • Pest & Disease Control: Monitor for common summer issues.
  • Trimming & Pruning: Maintain growth, especially before hurricane season.
  • Hurricane Prep: Secure loose items, trim vulnerable branches.

Fall (September - November)

  • Fertilization: Prepare plants for cooler weather.
  • Weed Control: Address fall germination.
  • Tree & Shrub Care: Pruning, potential planting of cool-season annuals.

Winter (December - February)

  • Light Pruning: Remove dead/diseased branches.
  • Cold Protection: Advise on covering sensitive plants during freezes.
  • Winter Planting: Ideal time for certain trees, shrubs, and cool-season flowers.

Spring (March - May)

  • Pre-emergent Application: Prevent summer weeds.
  • Seasonal Planting: Introduce warm-season annuals, new landscape elements.
  • Irrigation Check: Prepare for increased summer water needs.
  • Mulching: Retain moisture, suppress weeds.
